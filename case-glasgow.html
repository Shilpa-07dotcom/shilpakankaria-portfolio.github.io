Glasgo

A city companion that helps the 50,000+ students arriving in Glasgow each year discover the city and feel at home — turning an unfamiliar place into somewhere that feels yours.

01 · The Problem

A new city that's hard to call home

Limited local knowledge slows integration and leaves newcomers isolated.

New students often struggle with the city's layout, social venues and affordable options — which slows their integration in those crucial first weeks. Glasgo answers this with a single, accessible guide that connects students to Glasgow's vibrant community.

02 · The Solution

One companion for student city life

Discover, save, explore and connect — with a reason to keep coming back.

  • Interactive map — locate popular spots across Glasgow at a glance.
  • Curated recommendations — social venues, dining and cultural attractions.
  • Student-only offers — exclusive discounts that help newcomers save.
  • Real-time updates — stay informed about the latest events.
  • Gamification — earn rewards for exploring the city.
  • Accessibility — clear navigation and high-contrast visuals throughout.

Final UI

The experience, screen by screen

Onboarding

Friendly onboarding screens welcome users and get them set up in moments.

Gamification — the USP

Earn points by checking in at city locations via the interactive map. Points appear on a leaderboard, encouraging exploration and friendly competition.

Places, food & activities

Explore top spots, popular foods and activities — and even order food, all within the app.

Community & accessibility

Connect through groups, story highlights and messaging. Accessibility settings let users change language, adjust font sizes and switch between light and dark mode.

Glasgo, brought to life

Reflection

What I learned

Designing for belonging — not just utility — shaped every decision. The gamified check-in turned a practical map into a reason to explore, and accessibility was a first-class feature, not an afterthought. Glasgo taught me how much emotion drives adoption in a brand-new environment.
