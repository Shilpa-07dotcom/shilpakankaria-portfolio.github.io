About

Eight years in fashion taught me taste under constraint. UX gave that taste a system to live in.

I started in fashion design and merchandising — where every decision had to balance aesthetics, cost and commercial reality. That discipline carried straight into product design: I research deeply, but I never let research replace judgement.

Illustration and mural work keep my eye sharp — rhythm, composition, restraint. It shows up in every interface I ship.
